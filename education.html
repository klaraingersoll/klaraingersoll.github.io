Cambridge Rindge & Latin High School
Graduated June 2016. Cambridge, MA

Cambridge Rindge and Latin is where my interest in developing solutions to issues of inequality started.
I founded and led a feminism club 'Club 1,' to address sexism and misogyny on campus, by getting our school's dress code & sexual harassment policies (which were unfairly enforced and rooted in victim blaming) changed. I also founded and led a community initiative, "The Laptop Project" to address the digital divide at my school by getting laptops to students without adaquate computer access at home. I helped create a multi-media enviromental campaign, "Bottle Up," providing reuseable water bottles with a bottle-up logo (which I designed) to each student as well as making & airing informational videos on reducing plastic waste to promote health and sustainability on campus.
