LINK Arts x Action is a program I started to facilitate the connection between art and political action in the student movement at University of Massachusetts. I worked with the student gallery on campus to implement the program through partering with leaders of political organizations to lead art workshops, which I facilitated, helping artists plug in to the visioning and visualizing process of different pressing issues in the student movement. I’ve designed and lead the workshops: “Visualizing Safer Sex: “Making Art for a Collective Awakening on Sexual & Reproductive Health,” with the Students for Reproductive Justice, “Sign-a-thon for Walkout Against Right Wing Terror” with racial justice organizers and the International Socialist Organization, as well as "Heating Up for Debt Free Future’s Week of Action" with Students For a Debt Free Future.
