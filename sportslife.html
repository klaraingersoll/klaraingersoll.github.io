I created a plan for a digital campaign for A Bright Future, a book examining the path to decarbonization of the world's energy grid. As part of this, I compiled an accompanying list of influencers in the climate and clean-energy world, and conducted outreach with leading climate non-profits and think-tanks. I worked with an advisor from the Clean Air Task Force, as well as travelled to Washington D.C. to meet with representatives from the the Bipartisan Policy Center, Third Way, Boundary Stone Partners, and the Information Technology Innovation Foundation.
